Professor Shelton Peiris

Professor of Statistics & Financial Econometrics
School of Mathematics and Statistics
Faculty of Science
The University of Sydney
NSW 2006, Australia

I am a Fellow of the Royal Statistical Society (FRSS), an Honorary Fellow of the Institute of Applied Statistics, Sri Lanka (FIASSL), an elected member of the International Statistical Institute (ISI), and a World Class Professor at the University of Gadjah Mada, Indonesia.

My research examines time series analysis, financial econometrics, and statistical inference. I completed my PhD in Mathematical Statistics at Monash University in 1987.
